About Me

Hi! I'm Agustina and I'm about to graduate as an Audiovisual Designer at the University of Buenos Aires. I work as a marketing assistant and, in my free time, I teach photography, since I enjoy sharing my knowledge with others.
My understanding of audiovisual design allows me to apply the design thinking process everyday and has opened the doors to the world of digital marketing and, more recently, to UX Design.
My experience working in teams and leading them has allowed me to face various challenges and find the best way to solve them.
Currently, I am learning and improving my skills in web development and UX/UI design, and my goal is to focus my career on the latter area.
I am a person who is passionate about teamwork, continuous learning and teaching, I love talking and exchanging ideas, if you want to know more about me or would like us to talk, do not hesitate to contact me.
